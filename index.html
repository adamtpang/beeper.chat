the method

Not oldest-first. Importance × urgency.

Every chat gets two scores from 1–5. Their product (1–25) sets the order, so a partner's one-word text outranks a vendor's "URGENT!!!" blast. Then each is sorted into one move.

how it works

Run /beeper. Reach zero.

01

Reads everything

Pulls every unread chat across all your networks through the Beeper Desktop MCP, running locally on your machine.

WhatsAppiMessageSignalTelegramDiscord
02

Scores & sorts

Rates each chat importance × urgency (1–25) and classifies it: a reply to send, a task to do first, or pure noise.

03

Surfaces the top one

The most important chat first, as a context bundle: who it's from, the gist, what it knows about them, and a reply drafted in your voice.

04

You approve

Send, edit, skip, or archive. It never sends, reacts, or archives without your explicit OK, every time.

Every chat is a reply to send, or a task to do first.
